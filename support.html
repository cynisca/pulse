Frequently Asked Questions

From the home screen, tap the + button in the top right corner. You can either:

  1. Choose from a Starter Pack — curated sets of trackers for mood, health, fitness, productivity, and more. These give you a great starting point.
  2. Create a custom tracker — name it, pick a color, set the value type (rating, yes/no, number, etc.), and you're ready to go.

Trackers appear on your home screen and can be reordered by pressing and holding.

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find Pulse and tap it
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

You must cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date to avoid being charged for the next period. You will continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing cycle.

You can export your data in CSV or JSON format at any time:

  1. Open Pulse and go to Settings
  2. Tap Export Data
  3. Choose your preferred format (CSV or JSON)
  4. Share the file using any method available on your device (AirDrop, email, Files, etc.)

The export includes all of your trackers and their entries with timestamps. We recommend exporting periodically as a backup since all data is stored locally on your device.

To permanently delete all your data from Pulse:

  1. Open Pulse and go to Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete All Data
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Warning: This action is irreversible. All trackers, entries, and settings will be permanently removed. Consider exporting your data first. Alternatively, uninstalling the app will also delete all data.

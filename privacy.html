Privacy Policy

Effective February 15, 2026

The short version: Pulse is a local-only app. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

No Account Required

Pulse does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. You can start tracking immediately after downloading.

Local-Only Data Storage

All data you create in Pulse — including trackers, entries, reflections, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device. Your data is never uploaded to, processed by, or stored on any external server.

No Data Collection

Pulse does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking SDKs are included in the app
  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • No health, mood, or behavioral data leaves your device
  • No crash reporting data is collected by us
  • No third-party advertising SDKs are included

Subscription Purchases

Pulse offers an optional subscription for premium features. If you choose to subscribe:

  • Apple processes all payments through the App Store. Apple's privacy policy governs how they handle payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat is used to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives a anonymous app user ID and subscription status from Apple, but does not receive any of your tracked data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

No tracked data, mood entries, health metrics, or personal content is ever shared with Apple, RevenueCat, or any other third party.

No Third-Party Advertising

Pulse does not display advertisements and does not include any advertising SDKs or tracking pixels.

Data Deletion

Because your data is stored locally, you are in full control. You can delete all data at any time by navigating to Settings > Delete All Data within the app. Uninstalling the app will also remove all stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Pulse does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect any personal data from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of Pulse after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact
